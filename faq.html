Your questions answered!

What is the best time of year to visit Taniti?

The best time to visit Taniti is between May and September when the weather is dry and temperatures are mild. However, Taniti is a year-round destination with warm temperatures throughout the year.

Do I need a visa to visit Taniti?

Most visitors do not need a visa for stays up to 90 days. However, you should check with your country's embassy or consulate for the most up-to-date visa requirements before your trip.
